In the Spirit of the ancestors: Reconciling Post Tribal Stress Disorder

by Patrisia Gonzales

full Moon

With the full moon I plan on releasing the frustration that I feel from people that group all spanish speaking or brown people together. I can personally attest to having dealt with this due to my family coming from Chile. Growing up it was rare to find people that actually knew what chile was let alone find it on a map. Because of this I would always be categorized as mexican. While it did not bother me to be called Mexican it would have still felt better to have my true roots be recognized.

new Moon

With the new moon I wish to attract new and informed people on the various cultures and deviations of latinos. The reason for this is that I believe people can be quick to form patterns in their mind that might not actually exist. For example when I say that people don't know where Chile is I am only basing this on my own experiences which is not enough to truly form an opinion about an entire population. It could very much be the case that my situation is an anomaly or I have formed a pattern of people that categorize all spanish speakers as mexican and it could be the case that this isn't reality. Because of this I believe meeting people with these thought processes similar to my own would help better analyze people's true way of thinking rather than generalizing due to a few bad experiences.
